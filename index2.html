Swap Consulting is a Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) company based in the heart of Central India at Nagpur. We offer business and data processing services that match international quality standards while simultaneously providing significant cost-efficiencies. We specialize in the outsourcing of knowledge-intensive business processes that require domain expertise - also known as Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO).

Our core services :

  • Data Entry
  • Data Conversion and Digitization
  • Form Processing
  • Scanning & Indexing
  • Accounting & Legal services
  • Web Research
  • Non-voice customer support
  • Online bidding

Mission Statement

"To provide best-in-class BPO services to our clients, allowing them to focus on their core business with the assurance that their routine back-office processes will be run smoothly without compromising quality and reliability."

Our approach

  • Partnership approach : We partner with our clients in their pursuit of operational excellence and efficiencies

  • Cost-effectiveness : We help our clients be competitive in the global market by reducing the operational costs of outsourced processes

  • Process improvement : We don't stop at process implementation but go one step ahead and suggest improvements based on metrics

  • Small size:

    • Client-focused

    • Flexible

  • Cost effective

  • Scalability

  • Low employee turnover
