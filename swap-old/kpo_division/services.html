B2B Database Directory

We have a huge database of over 35,000 USA companies from different industries/location. We specialise in providing a database directory of US companies/organisations according to your preference.

We can provide with a list of contacts based on the following criteria :

  • Industry type
  • Business type
  • Designation/Department
  • Location

Once you select your criteria for the database you want to receive, we will update you with the number of contacts available in our directory, which in turn will help you select the right plan.

We provide ethically-sourced corporate email contacts of ONLY the top management officials of any company.

We search the data manually, and our data accuracy rate is 90%.

Your preferred data will be sent to you within minimum of 48 hours of payment. We will provide you with the database in an Excel sheet format or any other format that you may prefer.

Information included in our directories have the following :

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • E-mail
  • Designation
  • Company Name
  • Location/Address
  • Company Website
  • Industry Name
  • Other, if any

We can also create custom database for your company, according to your preference, of any industry type, location (anywhere country in the world), or any other criteria you may have.

We also help companies with their e-mail marketing utilising their e-mailing interface.
